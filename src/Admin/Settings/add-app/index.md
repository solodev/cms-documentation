Adding a new app to your Solodev CMS is quick and straightforward. Just fill out the form, and you'll be up and running in no time!

<p><img src="/static/images/settings/apps/add-app.jpg" alt="Add App"></p>

**Name** | **Description** 
:--- | ---
Name | Customize the app's name to make it easy to identify.
Location | Select the desired location where the app will be deployed. Options include Workspace, Organization, Support, and Admin.
Package	Upload | Upload the app package file. Supported formats may vary depending on your project requirements.

After filling in the required details, click Submit to finalize the process. Solodev CMS will take care of integrating your app seamlessly into the platform.
